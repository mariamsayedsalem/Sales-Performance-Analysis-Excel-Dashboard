## 📖 Project Overview
This project provides a comprehensive analysis of retail sales data across various regions and product categories. Using a dataset of nearly **10,000 orders**, I developed an interactive **Excel Dashboard** to visualize revenue distribution, regional performance, and product profitability.

---

## 🎯 Key Objectives
- 🧹 **Data Processing:** Cleaned and structured large-scale retail data (Superstore dataset).
- 🔄 **Pivot Analysis:** Aggregated data to compare performance across Categories (Technology, Furniture, Office Supplies).
- 📈 **Regional Mapping:** Analyzed sales distribution across Central, East, South, and West regions.
- 📊 **Dynamic Visualization:** Built an interactive interface using Slicers for real-time data filtering.

---

## 📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- ✅ **Total Sales:** Analyzed over **$2.26 Million** in total revenue.
- ✅ **Top Category:** **Technology** emerged as the leader with over **$827K** in sales.
- ✅ **Regional Leader:** The **West Region** recorded the highest sales volume (~$710K).
- ✅ **Data Scope:** Processed approximately **9,800 unique order entries**.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- **Microsoft Excel:** Pivot Tables, Power Query, and Advanced Charting.
- **Data Visualization:** Interactive Slicers and Dynamic Dashboards.

---

## 💡 Business Insights
- **Revenue Drivers:** Technology and Furniture are the primary revenue generators, contributing the highest value per order.
- **Market Distribution:** The West and East regions are the strongest markets, significantly outperforming the South and Central regions.
- **Order Volume:** Office Supplies have the highest transaction frequency, indicating high customer demand for smaller items.
